Hello Sponsors! HAkron 2.0 is right around the corner. Here is everything you need to know for the day of!

~ What to Bring ~

Venue: The Bit Factory, Suite 511, 526 S. Main Street Akron OH 44311

Enter the building and take the elevator up to the 5th floor. If you need to use the freight elevator, let us know ahead of time by email.

  1. A table cloth to fit a 6ft table
  2. Swag you want to hand out to the participants (t-shirts, waterbottles, pens, stickers, etc.)
  3. Promotional material for your company (posters, table runner, banners, etc.)
  4. A smile! :)

A map of where to park can be seen below (parking is first come, first serve basis). Green is the building and blue and yellow are where you can park. There is also a sponsors specifc schedule below. Sponors can show up as early as 9 am on Saturday to set up, but keep in mind that check-in for participants doesn't start until 11 am. On the day of, if you have any problems you can call or text Julie at (603)-548-8971.
